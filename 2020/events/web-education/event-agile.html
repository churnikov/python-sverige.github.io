Our customers don’t care that we are agile!

Live stream: https://youtu.be/MDxaYWv-c44

Abstract: Agile practises such as short iterations and reprioritizing continuously empower developers to deliver high quality software, but what about the needs of the stakeholders? They need to know when their problem is going to be solved. Does agile stand in the way of having a transparent and predictable workflow?

About the speaker: Sara Wänerskär is a Python developer who loves data and agile practises.
