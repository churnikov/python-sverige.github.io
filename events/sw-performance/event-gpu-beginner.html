A Beginners Guide to GPUs for Pythonistas

Live stream: https://youtu.be/5s8PljqLdkA

Abstract: In this talk, I will be discussing Python and GPUs! I will give a brief history of Python and parallel computing, take a look at Python’s ‘infamous’ GIL, and share how and why Pythonistas can get started with using GPUs to do more work faster.

About the speaker: Marlene Mhangami is the current Vice-Chair for the Python Software Foundation, Chair of PyCon Africa and co-founder of ZimboPy, a non-profit organisation that gives Zimbabwean young women access to resources in the field of technology.
She is currently studying Computer Science with the University of London and interning with Nividia, working on the RAPIDSAI project.
